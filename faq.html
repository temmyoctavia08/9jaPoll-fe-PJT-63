Frequently asked questions

General elections will be held in Nigeria on 23 February 2023 to elect the President, Vice President , House of Representatives and Senate. The winners of the election will be inaugurated on 29 May, 2023, the former date of Democracy Day.

As a new voter, you can pre-register online, or as a registered voter, you can review your voter registration, request to update your information or transfer your voter registration to another polling unit.

While it is desirous to give Nigerians abroad the right to vote, this is not currently possible. The actualisation requires some constitutional amendment and changes in the Electoral Act which has not been done.

To be eligible to vote you must be:

  • (i) A Nigerian citizen
  • (ii) Be 18 years and above
  • (iii) Possess a Permanent Voter Card
  • (iv) Be in the Register of Voters where he/she is assigned to vote

For further information on the elections please visit www.inecnigeria.org
